Towards a Kuposhan Mukt Bharat

Towards a Kuposhan Mukt Bharat is a series of episodes released weekly on YouTube. The series explores the status of child nutrition and maternal health in the country and discusses strategies Towards a Malnutrition Free India.  

For a weekly dose of policy analysis, research, & much more!

Lets work together for the better health & nutrition of Mothers and Children

We bring innovative ways to overcome the issues related to maternal and children health and nutrition

What we do?

Data Collection

Quality of the data collected has crucial role to play in overall sense-making out of it. We do provide training on data collection

Training on Effective Breastfeeding

Training during pregnancy creates awareness among mothers about the effective breastfeeding and underlying science behind it.

Data Analytics

Data is analyzed by the experts in our team to build useful insights through trend-analysis, pattern-findings etc.

Training of Front-line workers

Training of 3As (Anganwadi workers, ASHA & ANM) is important backbone of health & nutrition of mothers and children

Data Visualization

Data visualization ensures that insights are being conveyed in an effective way for smooth future action plan

Training of College Students

Capacity building by training students on various aspects of maternal & child health and nutrition
